Porsche Brand

Porsche Retail is about the Experience

Porsche has its brand strongly associated with luxurious, sporty racing cars that hold a significant place in popular culture. As its business develops, grasping its brand value and consumer demographics becomes instrumental in shaping the future.

Situated in Changsha, China, this Porsche Studio epitomizes a novel retail concept that centers on creating experiences and fostering a sense of community. The evolving retail landscape integrates the physical stores with the digital art , establishing itself as the world's first exclusive virtual art gallery initiated by an automotive brand.

Simultaneously, the cornerstone lies in comprehending the motivations of the local customer base. The adaptive space connects cafe, Porsche Experience, and Porsche Customization, providing options of Porsche owners' community event or engaging with a broader audience. Through demographic market research, we have discovered that Porsche's audience in China no longer fits the stereotypical image of Porsche enthusiasts but instead comprises a younger and increasingly female demographic community.

Design Strategies

In response to the dynamic and multifaceted landscape of contemporary retail, our design prioritizes adaptability. We strive to infuse flexibility and diverse atmospheres by leveraging cutting-edge technological components, offering a range of experiences through virtual environmental shifts. Rooted in Porsche's distinctive brand identity, this diversity awakens various senses and perceptions associated with Porsche, further elevating its brand relevance within popular culture among young people in China.

An Example for Dynamo Cloud Connection's Benefit

-- Calculate Door Fire Rating in Models to Meet Building Codes

In F.B.'s case, he would like to check Revit Data sheet with regional codes to make sure all doors meet the building fire codes. Here we compare three workflow with Revit, Dynamo, and Dynamo Cloud connection.

    [Revit] -- F.B. compared data cross-platform. He had Revit Schedule to view data from the 3D design file, and compared it with the imported building codes in Excel.   manual work low accuracy
    [Dynamo] -- The comparison is automated by in Dynamo where it reads data from Excel and compares within platform to check 3D model data.   automation high accuracy
    [Dynamo Cloud Connection] -- With Autodesk Cloud connected, multiple design files can be measured with a single click. Especially with projects in the same region where they share the same building codes. For fire code inspector, they can even reduce manual work to open, close, and run files through the Dynamo Batch automation.   automation high accuracy batch files

Collaboration & Community

Cross-Organization Collaboration

Having been a part of a large tech firm, I deeply valued was the abundance of resources and a pool of exceptional talents in the organization. As Dynamo functions as a cross-product and cross-platform automation tool, fostering seamless communication and collaboration across various teams becomes indispensable.

Throughout the course of my internship, we proactively engaged with platform service teams, working hand-in-hand to seamlessly integrate their web-based interfaces such as the Large Model Viewer and AEC Data Model into Dynamo. This collaboration aimed at delivering an enriched and immersive user experience. As the discussions unfolded and the teams ventured into exploration, I identified and suggested pertinent shortcuts based on insights garnered from lessons learned by other groups. This input notably contributed to the efficient reallocation of resources within the team.

    Special thanks to the following team who shared ideas with me and the Dynamo team.
    [PSET] Data Exchange Team, Authentication Team, Data Model Team, Large Model Viewer Team
    [COO] Solution Architect
    [AEC Design] Design Studio, User Research Team


Community

Dynamo's user community wins it in the competitive landscape. Community memebers share their insights and collectively troubleshoot issues in a creative design field.
