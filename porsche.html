Brand Research

Porsche Retail is about the Experience

Porsche has its brand strongly associated with luxurious, sporty racing cars that hold a significant place in popular culture. As its business develops, grasping its brand value and consumer demographics becomes instrumental in shaping the future. Through demographic market research, we have discovered that Porsche's audience in China no longer fits the stereotypical image of Porsche enthusiasts but instead comprises a younger and increasingly female demographic community.

Situated in Changsha, China, this Porsche Studio epitomizes a novel retail concept that centers on creating experiences and fostering a sense of community. The evolving retail landscape integrates the physical stores with the digital art , establishing itself as the world's first exclusive virtual art gallery initiated by an automotive brand.

Design Strategies

The New Retail Experience is Beyond Shopping

In response to the dynamic and multifaceted landscape of contemporary retail, our design prioritizes adaptability. We strive to infuse flexibility and diverse atmospheres by leveraging cutting-edge technological components, offering a range of experiences through virtual environmental shifts. Rooted in Porsche's distinctive brand identity, this diversity awakens various senses and perceptions associated with Porsche, further elevating its brand relevance within popular culture among young people in China.

Design Concept

Adaptivity in Porsche Cafe, Customization, and Experience areas

Attract:
Porsche Cafe

Porsche Café appeals to both car enthusiasts and coffee lovers. Even none of them describes you the best, it's a cool space to hangout and meet friends.

Seduce:
Porsche Customization

Wanna something personalized? Here we go. Have a Porsche car all by your preference. Interactive Car parts are displayed and customers can try on or compare at first glance in the customization section.

Convince:
Porsche Experience

With the hero car on display and virtual art gallery for the porsche community, Porsche Experience brought you the real driving experience and its community.

Simultaneously, the cornerstone lies in comprehending the motivations of the local customer base. The adaptive space connects Porsche cafe, Porsche Experience, and Porsche Customization, providing options of Porsche owners' community event or engaging with a broader audience.
As we design, we imagined multple scenarios that can serve more Porsche Owner Community's need and attract more potential customers, meanwhile, the space remain open for creative ideas with the curtain configurations.

Design Details

Wood Panels &
Silver cabinet

The walnut wood panels follow the Porsche AG guideline that unifies Porsche retails globally. The brushed silver cabinet introduces a sleek vibe and futuristic touch to the woody environment.

Curtain & Glass

With curtains for Adaptivity, interior material choices align with the visual language of curtain, the choice of glazed fluted glass recaptured the pattern of the curtain. Neutral touch of floor and ceiling sets them back and directs attention toward the central level, where activities take place and individuals interact.

Lighting

Special lighting object is designed for highlighting Porsche cars for guest experience.

Thoughts

Working with Clients

In this globalized world, we operate on an international scale. Our UNStudio is based in Amsterdam, and we collaborate with Porsche China in weekly meetings. Porsche China also delivered comments by Porsche AG in Germany. As the project evolved, we worked closely with dealers, specialized consultants, and contractors in Changsha. On the other sides, we suggested lighting and furniture manufacturer from Belgium and Italy. As architects, we played roles of coordinators and problem-solvers in this larger global team. We initiated the project from its inception, committing to achieving multiple milestones along the way.

Maintaining a two to three months' overview projection proved to be a valuable practice. Simultaneously, our team is highly dynamic, and team members are involved in multiple projects, requiring us to set priorities and engage at various stages of each project. Joined the team from the beginning, I've observed how our project manager expertly assembles a team that leverages each member's skillset and aligns it with the project's needs. I'm consistently inspired by the dynamism and the unique energy that each team member contributed.
